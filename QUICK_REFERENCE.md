## 🤖 AI Chatbot

### How It Works
1. **Click** blue chat icon (bottom-right, above WhatsApp)
2. **Type** your question or use quick replies
3. **Wait** for AI to process (shows "Thinking...")
4. **Get** intelligent, context-aware response
5. **Continue** conversation - AI remembers context

### What It Can Do
- Answer questions about VedTech services
- Provide contact information
- Guide users to appropriate pages
- Explain service offerings
- Direct to support channels
- Maintain professional conversation

### Technical Details
- **Powered by**: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash
- **Response Time**: 2-5 seconds
- **Availability**: 24/7
- **Context**: Remembers conversation history
- **Fallback**: Contact info if AI fails
